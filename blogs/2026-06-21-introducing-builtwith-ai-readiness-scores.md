AI is changing how companies build, publish, automate, integrate, and expose their digital infrastructure. It is also changing how other systems discover and interact with websites. BuiltWith now includes AI Readiness signals in website profiles and API responses, helping users understand how prepared a website appears to be for AI-driven discovery, automation, and agent interaction.
